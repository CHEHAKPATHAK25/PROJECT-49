Interesting facts about Camels :

Camel's ears are furry. Camel can move easily across the sand because of its specially designed feet. When they find water, they will drink as much as possible.


Interesting facts about Chameleons :

Chameleons are repltiles that are part of the Iguana Suborder. Changing skin color is an important part of communications among chameleons. Most chameleons have a prehensive toil that they use to wrap around tree branches.


Interesting facts about Pandas :

Pandas are good at climbing trees as well as they can also swim. Although panda's have carnivorous teeth, yet they eat bamboo and fruits. Pandas have six toes to grasp bamboo.


Interesting facts about Pigeons :

Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities. Pigeons can produce fake milk. They can fly in higher altitudes.
